George of the Jungle

Baby George is the lone survivor of a plane crash and is raised by a wise gorilla in the jungle. One day, he saves Ursula and moves from his sweet jungle life in Africa to San Francisco.

Release date: 16 July 1997 (USA)

Director: Sam Weisman

Music by: Marc Shaiman

Narrator: Keith Scott

Box office: $174.4 million
