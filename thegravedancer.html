The Grave Dancer

After the funeral of a friend, three friends break into a local cemetery and after reading an incantation on a tombstone, they all dance on the graves. Soon, they are haunted by 3 different ghosts.

Initial release: 15 July 2006

Director: Mike Mendez

Music by: Joseph Bishara

Written by: Brad Keene; Chris Skinner
